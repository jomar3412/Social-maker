# Fun Facts Style Guide

## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Friendly but authoritative - like a curious science teacher who's excited to share discoveries

**Personality:** We're the friend who reads Wikipedia for fun and can't wait to share what we learned

**Energy:** Medium-high. Not screaming, but genuinely enthusiastic

**Reading Level:** 8th grade - smart but accessible

---

## Hook Framework (First 5 Seconds)

The hook makes or breaks the video. It must stop the scroll immediately.

### Hook Types

| Type | Description | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Curiosity** | Make them need to know the answer | "Scientists still can't explain why..." |
| **Controversy** | Challenge what they think they know | "You've been lied to about..." |
| **Revelation** | Promise secret knowledge | "99% of people don't know this about..." |
| **Impossibility** | Present something that seems impossible | "This is the only animal that..." |

### Hook Templates

Use these as starting points:

- "Did you know that {fact}?"
- "Scientists still can't explain why {mystery}."
- "This is the only {thing} in the world that {does_what}."
- "You've been lied to about {topic}."
- "The real reason {thing} {does_what}..."
- "99% of people don't know this about {topic}."
- "This one {thing} changed {outcome} forever."
- "What happens to {thing} will shock you."

### Hook Rules

- Max 12 words
- Active voice only
- Present tense
- No setup - straight to the hook
- Must create an "information gap"

## Vocabulary

### Power Words (Use Often)

| Category | Words |
|----------|-------|
| Authority | scientists, discovered, proven, research, study |
| Mystery | secret, hidden, mystery, unknown, ancient |
| Impact | shocking, impossible, never, only, first, last |
| Rarity | rare, extinct, forbidden, banned, unique |

### Forbidden Words (Never Use)

- literally, basically, like, um, uh
- you know, so yeah, anyway
- kind of, sort of, pretty much
- actually (except as a reveal)
- just, really, very (weak intensifiers)

### Emphasis Words (Use Sparingly)

Use ALL CAPS for maximum impact on ONE word per video:
- ONLY, NEVER, ALWAYS, EVERY, ZERO

## Content Structure

**Total Duration:** 45 seconds (30-59 acceptable)
**Max Words Per Sentence:** 10

### Section Breakdown

| Section | Duration | Words | Purpose |
|---------|----------|-------|---------|
| Hook | 5 sec | 12 | Stop the scroll |
| Setup | 10 sec | 25 | Context, why this matters |
| Reveal | 20 sec | 50 | The actual fact with proof |
| Kicker | 10 sec | 25 | Mind-blowing implication + CTA |

### Pacing Rules

- No sentence longer than 10 words
- Vary sentence length for rhythm
- Pause after major reveals
- Build tension before the payoff

---

## Content Categories

Focus on these high-performing categories:

1. **Science** - Space, physics, biology, chemistry
2. **Nature** - Animals, plants, weather, geography
3. **Human Body** - Anatomy, psychology, health
4. **History** - Ancient civilizations, wars, discoveries
5. **Technology** - Inventions, innovations, future tech

### Content Rules

- Facts MUST be true and verifiable
- Include source when possible ("According to NASA...")
- Avoid recently debunked myths
- Prefer visual facts (things you can show)

---

## Visual Style

### Text on Screen

Always show:
- Key numbers ("3 hearts", "200 million years")
- Comparisons ("10x larger than")
- Main keywords ("octopus", "NASA", "discovery")

### Effects

- **Zoom** on key words
- **Shake** on major reveals
- **Glow** on numbers
- **Whoosh** sound on transitions
- **Hit** sound on emphasis

## CTA Templates

End every video with an engagement hook:

- "Follow for more mind-blowing facts."
- "Which fact should I cover next?"
- "Comment if you knew this already."
- "Save this for later."
- "Part 2 coming soon..."

## Quality Checklist

Before posting, verify:

- [ ] Hook is under 12 words
- [ ] All sentences under 10 words
- [ ] No filler words
- [ ] Fact is accurate and sourced
- [ ] Visual cues for every key point
- [ ] Sound effects on transitions
- [ ] Strong CTA at the end
- [ ] Relevant hashtags included
- [ ] Duration between 30-59 seconds
